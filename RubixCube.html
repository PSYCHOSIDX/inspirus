Rubix Cube
Rules
  • Competitors must bring their own puzzles for the competition (3x3 Rubik's cube).
  • Puzzles must be clean, and must not have any markings, elevated pieces, damage, or other differences that significantly distinguish any piece from a similar piece.
  • All solves will be timed on the computer.
  • An inspection time of 15 seconds would be given.
  • An average of 3 will be taken.
  • If 2 or more participants have the same average time, there would be a tie-breaker round.
  • While competing, competitors must not use any electronics or audio equipment (e.g. cell phones, MP3 players, Dictaphones, additional lighting).
