Eligibility Criteria
  • Eligibility: Undergraduate and Diploma Students from professional and non-professional institutions.
  • Maximum numbers of participants: 25
  • Judging Criteria: Creativity, Originality, Storytelling
    • Rules
    • The drawing shall be in the form of a DIGITAL comic strip. The comic can be either 3-8 frames long. Participants have to create a comic by mashing characters from pop culture in an original story environment.
    • Participants will have 3 hours to draw the comic using the hardware provided by the institute i.e. Keyboard and Mouse. The software used for this event will be Photoshop.
    • Participants will select their characters to draw before the event in a lucky draw. They will be allowed to browse the internet for inspiration for some time before starting to draw. However, any candidate found having directly plagiarized their drawing will be disqualified immediately.
    • The drawing should not contain any lewd content. All such entries will be disqualified.
    • Further details will be given before the event.
    • Participants must follow all SOP's within the college.
    • In case of any disputes or discrepancies, the decision made by the Organizing Committee will be final.
