Keyboard Warriors
Rules
  • Participants will be sent links to join private rooms where the competition will be held on typrx.com
  • Prior to the event, participants have to log in with their personal Google accounts on the website.
  • Each private lobby will host at most 5 participants with a spectator.
  • They will be given a paragraph to type out and those who progress to the next round will be decided as per the results.
  • Participants should familiarise themselves with the website.
  • A demo race will be conducted by the event coordinators in a Google Meet with all the participants, prior to the event.
  • During the event, participants will also have to join a Google Meet and switch their cameras on.
  • Participants are requested to take a screenshot of their results at the end of each race.
  • Students participating must have a stable internet connection.
  • Assistance from another person would strictly not be permitted and the participant would immediately be disqualified if found doing so.
  • Participation Limit: 3 per college ( 1 per branch for DBCE students)
