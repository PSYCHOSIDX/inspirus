Inspirathon
Rules
  • Team members of each team should be from the same college (Interdepartmental teams are allowed).
  • Teams can use any programming language or software of their choice.
  • Teams have to carry their own laptops.
  • Problem statements will be released at _________. All the statements will be mailed to the teams by the coordinators.
  • Teams have to choose 1 problem statement by _____________, and have to inform the coordinators about the chosen problem statement.
  • Only 2 teams will be allocated with the same problem statement and the allocation process will take place on FIRST COME FIRST SERVE basis. Thereafter that particular statement will no longer be available for the rest of the participants.
  • All work on the project should be done at the Hackathon.
  • Wi-Fi access and power points will be available.
  • Teams have to report at the venue at least 30 minutes prior to the start of the hackathon.
  • Refreshments will be provided.
  • Hackathon will begin at _________ and will end at ___________(48 hrs). The teams are free to take breaks in between.
  • The teams will not be provided with any additional accommodation.
  • Teams are not allowed to leave the venue until the end of the hackathon.
  • The judging will begin after _____________.
  • Each team will be given 15-20 minutes to present their developed websites/apps to the judges in a presentation format.
  • In case of discrepancy, decisions made by the judges will be final.
  • All team members should carry with them their vaccination certificate (hard copy or soft copy) otherwise they will have to sign a declaration.
  • All team members are requested to follow all the Safety Protocols during the entirety of the event.
