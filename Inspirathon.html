Inspirathon
Eligibility Criteria
  • Eligibility Criteria: Undergraduate and Diploma Students from professional and non-professional institutions
  • For Don Bosco College of Engineering Participants Only
  • Only 2 teams from B.E. Computers belonging to Don Bosco College of Engineering will be allowed to participate.
  • Teams from S.E and T.E Computers are not allowed to participate.
  • Judging Criteria :Complexity, Solution to problem, Execution, Optimisation, Presentation.
Rules
  • Team members of each team should be from the same college (Interdepartmental teams are allowed).
  • Teams can use any programming language or software of their choice.
  • Teams have to carry their own laptops.
  • Online Team Registration will be closed 2 days before the event (27th October).
  • Offline on the spot Registration will also be available for the participants (This will close 30 minutes prior to the start of the event).
  • Problem statements will be released on 28th October. All the statements will be mailed to the teams by the coordinators.
  • Teams have to choose 1 problem statement by 29th October, and have to inform the coordinators about the chosen problem statement.
  • Only 2 teams will be allocated with the same problem statement and the allocation process will take place on FIRST COME FIRST SERVE basis. Thereafter that particular statement will no longer be available for the rest of the participants.
  • All work on the project should be done at the Hackathon.
  • Wi-Fi access and power points will be available.
  • Teams have to report at the venue at least 30 minutes prior to the start of the hackathon.
  • Refreshments will be provided.
  • Hackathon will begin at 12pm on 29th October and will end at 1pm on 30th October. The teams are free to take breaks in between.
  • The teams will not be provided with any additional accommodation.
  • Teams are not allowed to leave the venue until the end of the hackathon.
  • The judging will begin after 1pm on 30th October.
  • Each team will be given 15-20 minutes to present their developed websites/apps to the judges in a presentation format.
  • In case of discrepancy, decisions made by the judges will be final.

  • NOTE:
  • Clear Instructions will be given to the teams on how to upload their code on Github
